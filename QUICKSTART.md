## Step 3: Open on Your Phone

1. **Connect your phone to the same WiFi** as your computer
2. **Open the Network URL** (e.g., `http://192.168.1.100:3000/`) in your phone's browser
3. **Accept motion permission** when prompted

## Step 4: Make Music! ðŸŽµ

1. Tap **"Start Theremin"**
2. Allow motion/orientation access
3. Tilt your phone:
   - **Left/Right** = Change pitch
   - **Forward/Back** = Change volume
4. Experiment with different waveforms!
